About Recoup

Most recovery apps focus on social support or maintaining a streak, but Recoup focuses on organizing information and resources for the individual, giving you more insight and control over your own journey with guilt-free tools. Our relapse impulse tracker allows individuals to record an urge to use quickly and discreetly, and presents actionable analytics with the data entered. The Recoup button is always accessible, so even if the people the consumer trusts are out of reach, they have effective methods of redirection and grounding themselves at their fingertips. Between curating a custom Recoup procedure and the amount of information they have the option to log, individuals are going to get out of it what they put in, but they will never be punished for not using the app, having the urge to relapse, or physically relapsing.
